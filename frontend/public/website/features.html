Fleet Intelligence

Real-time awareness, location analytics and deep vehicle diagnostics to keep your fleet safe and efficient.

01

Live GPS Tracking

Get sub-second position updates for every vehicle in your fleet — simultaneously. FleetOS Pro streams telemetry directly from our cellular IoT network to your dashboard without polling delays.

  • 2-second refresh rate with WebSocket streaming
  • Live traffic overlay from HERE Maps / Google Maps
  • Cluster view for large fleets with heatmap density
  • Street-level satellite and hybrid map layers
  • Filter map by vehicle status, branch or driver group
app.fleetospro.com/map
02

Trip Playback

Replay any completed journey in full fidelity. Scrub through the timeline to review speed violations, stops, harsh events and route deviations. Invaluable for investigations, coaching and compliance reporting.

  • Frame-by-frame or variable-speed playback (1x–20x)
  • Harsh braking / acceleration events flagged on timeline
  • Stop duration and location shown inline
  • Export trip as PDF report or KML file
  • 365-day trip history retention
app.fleetospro.com/playback
03

Geofencing

Define unlimited virtual zones on the map — depots, customer sites, restricted areas or entire cities. Receive instant alerts when any vehicle crosses a zone boundary, lingers beyond a dwell limit, or enters outside permitted hours.

  • Draw polygon, circle or corridor zones
  • Schedule zones (active only during business hours)
  • Assign zones to vehicle groups or individual vehicles
  • Dwell time alerts and overstay warnings
  • Zone-based report filtering and heatmaps
app.fleetospro.com/geofences
04

Driver Performance Scoring

Transform driving behaviour with data-driven coaching. FleetOS Pro scores every driver on harsh braking, rapid acceleration, speeding, cornering, phone usage and idling — then generates automated coaching reports.

  • 0–100 composite safety score per driver per day
  • Driver league table with trend charts
  • In-cab buzzer alert via Bluetooth dongle
  • Monthly performance summary emailed to drivers
  • Integration with HR systems for incentive programmes
app.fleetospro.com/drivers
05

Vehicle Health Monitoring

OBD-II diagnostics read engine fault codes, battery voltage, coolant temperature, tyre pressure and fuel level in real time. DTC fault codes are decoded with plain-language descriptions so your workshop team knows exactly what's wrong before the vehicle arrives.

  • 3,000+ DTC fault codes decoded
  • Battery health trend with predictive alerts
  • Engine temperature and oil pressure monitoring
  • Tyre pressure sensor integration
06

Unauthorised Usage Detection

Protect your assets from misuse and theft. Detect after-hours ignition events, unexpected movements or trips without a paired driver ID. FleetOS Pro can trigger immobiliser commands and send instant alerts to fleet managers and security teams.

  • After-hours movement alerts with escalation
  • Driver ID pairing via RFID / NFC / PIN
  • Remote immobiliser command (where fitted)
  • Tamper detection and device removal alerts

Operations & Cost Control

Streamline dispatch, reduce fuel spend and keep vehicles maintained with AI-powered operational tools.

07

AI Route Optimisation

The FleetOS routing engine uses machine learning to compute optimal multi-stop routes considering live traffic, driver hours-of-service regulations, vehicle load capacity, customer time windows and priority stops. Typical fleets see 18–24% fewer kilometres driven within the first month.

  • Dynamic re-routing when traffic conditions change
  • Compliance with HOS and tachograph rules
  • Drag-and-drop dispatch with route preview
  • Integration with your existing TMS / ERP
08

Maintenance Scheduling

Never miss a service again. FleetOS Pro tracks odometer, engine hours and calendar dates to automatically schedule maintenance. The workshop calendar shows upcoming jobs, and automated reminders go to fleet managers and drivers well in advance.

  • Triggered by km, hours or fixed date
  • Full workshop history with part and labour costs
  • Downtime KPI tracking
  • Predictive failure alerts based on engine data
09

Customer Management

Manage customer accounts, delivery addresses and SLA agreements in one place. Share live ETA links with end customers, capture e-signatures on delivery, collect NPS feedback and escalate exceptions automatically.

  • Customer portal with live tracking links
  • E-signature and photo proof of delivery
  • NPS and rating capture post-delivery
  • SLA breach alerting and reporting
10

Fuel & Cost Tracking

Connect fuel cards from 40+ providers or enter fuel transactions manually. FleetOS Pro cross-references GPS idling data with fill-up records to detect fuel card fraud, calculate cost per kilometre by vehicle and driver, and identify inefficient routes.

  • Idle fuel cost calculator ($/hr by engine)
  • Fuel card fraud detection via GPS cross-check
  • Cost/km breakdown by vehicle, driver, route
  • Carbon footprint reporting
11

Smart Alert System

Configure alerts for over 40 trigger conditions — from simple geofence entry to complex multi-condition logic. Deliver alerts via SMS, email, push notification or webhook to integrate with your existing NOC tools.

  • 40+ configurable alert triggers
  • Multi-channel delivery: SMS, email, push, webhook
  • Alert escalation chains and suppression windows
  • Impact detection (accident alert with GPS co-ordinates)
12

IoT Device Management

Provision GPS trackers, temperature sensors, dashcams, fuel probes and BLE asset tags from a single console. Monitor device health, signal strength, battery level and firmware version across your entire hardware estate.

  • OBD-II, hardwired and asset trackers
  • Temperature and cold-chain monitoring
  • Remote firmware OTA updates
  • SIM management with data usage dashboard

Analytics & Reporting

Turn raw telematics data into actionable intelligence with a nine-report suite and executive dashboards.

13

Advanced Analytics

Custom date range queries, multi-dimensional pivots and segment comparisons. Build and save views tailored to your KPIs — then schedule delivery to your inbox.

  • Drag-and-drop KPI widget builder
  • Multi-dimension pivots by vehicle, driver, branch, route
  • Export as PDF, CSV or API feed
  • Scheduled email delivery — daily, weekly or monthly
app.fleetospro.com/reports
14

9-Report Suite

Pre-built reports: fleet utilisation, driver behaviour, trip summaries, maintenance history, fuel consumption, geofence activity, idle time, cost tracking and exception events. Ready to export as PDF or CSV.

15

Scheduled Report Delivery

Any report can be auto-generated and emailed on a daily, weekly or monthly cadence. Deliver to multiple stakeholders with tailored report profiles and access levels.

16

Executive Dashboard

C-suite KPI summary: total fleet cost, utilisation rate, safety score trend, maintenance compliance and top cost-saving opportunities — all on one screen at a glance.

17

Global Monitoring

Aggregate fleet metrics across regions and branches. Switch context in one click and drill into any branch for detail. Ideal for multi-country enterprise operations.

18

Cost Tracker

Total cost of ownership per vehicle: fuel, insurance, maintenance, driver allocation. Inform replacement cycles and CAPEX planning with hard data, not guesswork.

+

API & Webhooks

Push telematics data and analytics to your existing BI tools, ERP or TMS via REST API and webhook. Full OpenAPI spec included. SDKs available for Python, JS and Go.

Role-Based Access Control

Define granular permission sets for every module — vehicles, drivers, reports, billing, settings. Create custom roles beyond the built-in Fleet Manager, Driver, Dispatcher and Viewer roles. Assign per-branch scoping.

SOC 2 Control

Multi-Factor Authentication

Enforce MFA for all users or specific roles. Supports TOTP authenticator apps (Google Authenticator, Authy), SMS OTP and hardware security keys. Trusted device management reduces friction for regular users.
